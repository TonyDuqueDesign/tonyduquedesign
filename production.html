Production Art

As a production artist, I worked with an art/creative director to execute their vision into a variety of pieces while maintaining the original concept. I developed an eye for detail and the technical know-how to get the job done. I worked with a variety of design programs (DeBabelizer, QuarkXPress, InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, etc.) and ensured the artwork was production-ready and projects were finished on time. I developed layout skills, typography skills, file categorizing skills, and excellent organizational abilities.
